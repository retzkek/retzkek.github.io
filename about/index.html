Interests

  • I am an avid gamer, some of my favorite games recently are Borderlands 2, Mass Effect 3, Eve Online, Team Fortress 2, and Dota 2.
  • In an effort to lose weight (so far successful, but still a ways to go) I have become an adherent to a low carb or ketogenic (keto) or paleo-ish diet. While every body is different, mine certainly performs better when carbohydrates are kept to a minimum. This, along with StrongLifts 5x5 and a standing desk have become my recipe for a healthier me.
